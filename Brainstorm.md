# Splash Screen Project
My inspiration comes from a few different sources. Most of my ideas come from my history of videogames, books, and movies I have consumed. I love taking disparate ideas from my past and mixing them together to create interesting things. It's how I became a Dungeon Master for my Dungeons and Dragons group. I use that past experience of combined dissimilar mediums to create unique combinations. All of this culminated in the concept of my current game: Story game. Basically the idea is to make thematic and expository choices to make a character more powerful in order to defend a fictional world from an external threat. The choices you make change the story. At the end you get to see what story you made based on the choices you chose to make a more powerful character.
